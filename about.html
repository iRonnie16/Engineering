About US:

At A&W Engineering we manufacture and supply cattle farming and agricultural supplies. We specialise in cattle trailers, front loaders and buckets. We are based in Barrowhouse, Laois, Ireland. We began in late 2015 but have recently been growing fast. We have taken on more employees and plan to keep growing.

We work together with a number of independent engineering companies to bring you individually specialised parts for every product we sell to you.

We are NSAI approved meaning you can trust the safety and quality of every one of the products that we have available.

We have an ever increasing reputation since we first began and have loyal returning customers. We are still growing and with your help we hope to continue that!
